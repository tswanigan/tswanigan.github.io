About Me!

Hello, my name is Taylor Swanigan. I am a current sophomore at the University of Missouri-Columbia. I graduated from St. Louis, MO high school, Rosati-Kain in 2021. Currently, I am pursuing my Bachelors in Science and anticipate graduation in May of 2025. I am on the Pre-Medical track, majoring in Biology with a minor in Psychology. I have plans to attend medical school in hopes of becoming a top-neurosurgeon. When I am not studying, I hold the chair as Treasurer for a minority book club at Mizzou and attend biweekly MAPS-Minority Association for Pre-Health Students-meetings. I am currently conducting research under Dr. Lei Lei here at Mizzou. We focus on mammalian ovarian reserve formation maintenance, and its associated female reproductive health issues.
