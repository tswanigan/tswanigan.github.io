Home Page!

“Is that knitting or crochet?” Welcome to your new best guide on how to crochet. From the very straightforward materials to beginner stitches, let me assure you that crocheting is the right hobby for you so you can be on your way to making great things!

Read Me First!

Hi, my name is Taylor Swanigan! On this webpage, I will debunk any uncertainties you may have about crocheting. Learning a new hobby can feel intimidating in the beginning, but my goal is to make you confident as you pursue this craft. Throughout the website you will find: what exactly crochet is, how I learned and experienced this far, my recommended crochet tools & supplies for beginners, FAQS, a straightforward, step-by-step tutorial on how to crochet a row of single crochet stitches, and more! The navigation bar will allow you to find these things quickly. This webpage welcomes beginners, intermediates, even advanced crocheters. The goal is to refresh and learn how to crochet. Having fun while pursuing this hobby is a huge focus. Patience and perhaps even contacting me (listed on contact page) will set all your worries aside. You WILL get the hang of things quickly.
